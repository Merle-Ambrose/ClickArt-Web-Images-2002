Saving Instructions

Internet Explorer Users: To save an image, click on the various options until your selection narrows to a single image displayed. Then, right-click on the image and select the "Save Picture As..." option from the pop-up menu to save it to your hard drive.

Netscape Users: To save an image, right-click on the image and select the "Save Image..." option from the pop-up menu to save it to your hard drive.
